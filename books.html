GOOD READS

Surviving Mars

This book is the work of highly acclaimed author Willis Dudd and was published in 2020. It is an apocalyptic story of an austronaut who is on Mars when dooms day beckons and has no home to go back to.

Trouble In Paradise

This fictional book written by Richard Ramirez and published in 2005 is about a vacation gone terribly wrong. Mimi is on vacation in Jamaica. While on a visit to a bank, armed robbers attack it and take hostages, including Mimi.
